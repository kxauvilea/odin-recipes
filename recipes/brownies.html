chocolate brownies

this is an extra chocolatey brownie recipe that has been passed onto me from one of my friends! i hope you will enjoy this as much as i enjoy eating it :3

ingredients

  • 1 cup of butter, aka 2 sticks or half a pound
  • 2 cups of sugar, 1 cup brown sugar and 1 cup cane sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 cup of cocoa powder
  • one cup of chocolate chips

steps

  1. preheat oven to 325 degrees
  2. mix sugar and salt into slightly melted butter, you can microwave the butter for around 15 seconds if needed!
  3. fold in eggs one at a time
  4. add vanilla extract
  5. sift in flour, and then cocoa powder
  6. fold in chocolate chips
  7. transfer into baking dish
  8. bake for about 45 minutes
and enjoy!!
