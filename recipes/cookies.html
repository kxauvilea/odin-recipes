chocolate chip cookies

this is a super simple and easy chocolate chip cookie recipe! i've been using this recipe since i was 10, and always comes out amazing, i hope you enjoy!
recipe credits to geronimo stilton's pheonix of destiny lol

ingredients

  • 1/2 cup softened butter
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla
  • 1 and 1/4 cup flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

steps

  1. preheat the oven to 375 degrees F
  2. beat the sugar and butter together until creamy
  3. add egg, vanilla, and salt, continue mixing
  4. combine flour and baking soda in another bowl, then sift into the egg mixture
  5. add chocolate chips!
  6. scoop out balls of cookie dough onto your baking dish, 2-3 tbsp reccomended
  7. cook at 375 F for 8-10 minutes
lastly, enjoy!!
